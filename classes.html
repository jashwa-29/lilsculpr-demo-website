Online and Offline Clay Modelling Classes in Chennai

Lil Sculpr offers structured online and offline clay modelling classes in Chennai for kids, teens, and adults. Our courses are designed to improve creativity, focus, fine motor skills, and artistic confidence through hands-on learning.

From basic clay shapes to advanced miniature sculptures, students learn step by step under expert guidance in a fun and friendly environment.

We provide a wide range of clay modelling courses suitable for beginners and advanced learners. Parents can choose classes based on age, interest, and skill level.

Online & Offline Learning Options

Online Live Classes

  • Live guided sessions with expert instructors
  • Learn from the comfort of your home
  • Interactive and engaging virtual classes
  • Flexible scheduling options

Offline Studio Classes

  • Hands-on learning at our Chennai studio
  • All materials and tools provided
  • Small batch sizes for personal attention
  • Safe and child-friendly environment

Parents can choose what suits their child best.
