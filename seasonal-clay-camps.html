Seasonal Clay Camps

Make your holidays extra creative with our Seasonal Clay Camps — fun-filled programs where kids dive into clay sculpting, painting, and storytelling. Each day brings a new project that keeps children engaged, inspired, and excited to create something amazing.

Perfect for school breaks, these camps are designed to help kids explore creativity, build focus, and improve fine motor skills — all while having a blast! From cute characters to themed art pieces, every day is a new clay adventure.

Camp Activities Include:

  • Daily themed clay sculpting projects
  • Painting and color-mixing sessions
  • Creative storytelling through art
  • Team activities and fun games
  • Final-day showcase of creations

Why Join Our Clay Camps?

Our Lil Sculpr Instructor Team ensures that every child enjoys a balance of learning and fun. With hands-on guidance, personalized attention, and a joyful environment, kids develop both artistic and social skills while creating lasting memories this holiday season.
