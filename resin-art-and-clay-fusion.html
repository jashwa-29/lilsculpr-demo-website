Resin Art & Clay Fusion Class

Unleash your creativity by blending the elegance of resin art with the charm of handcrafted clay! This fusion course introduces you to both mediums, guiding you through the process of designing and crafting stunning functional and decorative pieces.

From shimmering jewellery and glossy coasters to intricate trays and wall décor, each session encourages exploration and experimentation. You’ll learn resin pouring, pigment mixing, embedding clay designs, and finishing techniques — creating unique art that stands out in both home décor and small business collections.

What You’ll Learn:

Gain in-depth knowledge of resin art and its perfect harmony with sculpted clay. You’ll explore composition, color balance, and safe resin-handling techniques while developing your own artistic style.

  • Introduction to resin types, pigments, and safety practices
  • Designing and sculpting clay elements for resin inclusion
  • Pouring, layering, and embedding decorative materials
  • Finishing, sanding, and polishing for professional quality
  • Creative guidance for building a handmade product line

Why Join This Class?

This hands-on class is ideal for craft lovers, aspiring entrepreneurs, and art enthusiasts who want to explore a modern creative medium. With expert mentorship from our Lil Sculpr Resin Artist, you’ll gain the confidence and skill to turn your passion into professional-looking creations — whether for gifting, display, or selling online.
