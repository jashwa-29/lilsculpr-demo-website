Clay Business Masterclass

Transform your creative passion into a thriving business with our Clay Business Masterclass! This 4-day program is designed for artists who want to turn their handmade clay creations into a professional brand and income source.

Guided by our Lil Sculpr Business Coach, you’ll gain the knowledge and tools needed to start and scale your art business — from pricing and marketing to packaging and presentation. Learn how to effectively sell through Instagram, Etsy, and craft fairs, and understand what makes your art stand out in the marketplace.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Pricing strategies for handmade clay products
  • Branding and packaging tips to attract buyers
  • Photography techniques for online listings
  • Setting up and managing Instagram & Etsy shops
  • Sales insights for art fairs and exhibitions

Why Join This Masterclass?

This course bridges the gap between creativity and entrepreneurship. You’ll not only refine your artistic presentation but also learn practical business techniques to help your clay creations reach a wider audience. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced artist, this masterclass will give you the confidence to grow your brand and sell successfully.
