Our Clay Modelling Programs

Nurturing Creativity in Young Artists (Ages 3-15)

Level 1-3: Basic Shapes

Perfect for beginners! Kids learn fundamental techniques like rolling, pinching, and shaping to create simple forms and characters.

Level 4-6: Intermediate Sculpting

Building on basics, students create more complex forms, animals, and simple structures with improved detail and technique.

Level 7-9: Advanced Techniques

Students master complex sculpting methods, texture creation, and begin working on multi-piece projects with professional finishing.

Level 10-12: Master Sculptor

Our highest level focuses on realistic miniatures, detailed sculptures, and portfolio development following international standards.

About Lil Sculpr

India's First Clay Modelling Academy for Kids
