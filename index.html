Our Clay Modelling Programs for Kids

Kids Clay Modelling Classes in Chennai – Step by Step Learning (Ages 5–15)

Level 1–3: Basic Clay Shapes for Kids

In these beginner levels, kids learn basic clay modelling shapes like balls, coils, hearts, leaves, fruits, and vegetables. These levels help children understand clay handling and build a strong foundation for clay modelling.

Level 4–6: Intermediate Clay Modelling for Kids

At this stage, kids move on to simple household objects, aquarium models, and pet animals. These levels improve shape control, creativity, and confidence in clay modelling classes for kids.

Level 7–9: Advanced Clay Sculpting for Kids

Kids learn advanced clay modelling by creating wild animals, planetarium models, and realistic miniature food items. These levels focus on detailing, textures, and creative thinking.

Level 10–11: Master Clay Sculpting Program

In the advanced stage, students create trees, forests, plants, human figurines, and god idols. These levels help kids develop professional sculpting skills and artistic expression through clay art.

Lil Sculpr offers structured kids clay modelling classes in Chennai with a level-based syllabus designed to grow along with your child’s creativity.
